This website contains informations about my research, myself and interests. [Full CV]

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Université de Caen-Normandie/France with Prof. Jalal Fadili in the Greyc Laboratory.

Interests: Optimization(convex, non-convex/smooth, non-smooth), Bregman-based algorithms, inverse problems and computational Optimal Transport.

Before that, I graduated from Université Paris-Saclay/France with the scholarship Sophie-Germain.
I have a Master's degree in Applied Mathemetics with a focus on optimization. I did my M2 internship with
Léonard Monsaingeon and Thomas Gallouet on Computational optimal transport inside the MOKAPLAN team.
